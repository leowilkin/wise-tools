Wise Tools

Version 1.1.0 (Firefox)
This extension adds a "Copy ID" button to Wise recipient pages, making it easy to copy recipient IDs to your clipboard.
How to use:
  1. Navigate to any recipient page on Wise
  2. Look for the orange "Copy ID" button next to Send and Delete
  3. Click it to copy the recipient's ID to your clipboard
  4. The button will briefly show "Copied!" when successful
